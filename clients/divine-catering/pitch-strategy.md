## 🗺️ Meeting Flow — Exactly what to say and when

**Opening (2 min)**
Pull out your phone or laptop and open the dashboard. Say: *"I prepared a quick digital intelligence audit of Divine Catering specifically — not a template, actual data from your Instagram and local search. Let me show you."*

Don't pitch anything yet. Let them look.

**Part 1: Intelligence (2 min)**
Point to the SWOT analysis and Audience Insights. Say: *"The good news is, your food and events look incredible. You have great reels, and clearly, you deliver high-quality traditional food. The problem is that all this great content lives in a silo on Instagram, where engagement is low, and people can't easily find your menu or book you."*

**Part 2: Revenue Leak Detection (3 min)**
This is where you activate the pain. Walk through the leaks. Say: *"We ran a diagnostic and found several active 'revenue leaks' where you are losing business to competitors."*
*   **Leak 1:** Focus heavily on the "No Local SEO". *"Right now, when someone searches 'wedding catering in Karuvanchal', you don't exist on Google. That is the highest-intent traffic possible."*
*   **Leak 2:** Point out the friction. *"Even if they find you on Instagram, there's no digital menu, no packages listed, and no simple way to inquire. They have to DM and wait. You are losing corporate clients and busy brides who want immediate info."*

**Part 3: The Cost of Inaction (2 min)**
This is your pressure moment. Say: *"The upcoming Wedding Season and corporate event rushes are approaching fast. If you don't have a dedicated digital infrastructure to capture leads, every rupee you spend on food, staff, and Instagram marketing is leaking out of the bucket."*

**Pivot to the Solution (1 min)**
Say: *"The good news is you have the content and the reputation. What's missing is the infrastructure. That's exactly what my studio builds."*

**Part 4: The Fast-Action Close (3 min)**
Show them the Commercials section. 
Say: *"Because the Wedding Season is about to start, we are taking on only 2 catering projects this month to guarantee delivery before the rush."*

Point to the **Fast-Action Bonus** box.
Say: *"If we start this week to ensure you are live for the upcoming wedding season, we will apply a **₹5,000 launch discount** on the Growth package, and we'll completely waive the setup fees for your Google Business Profile and automated WhatsApp booking integration."*

**Close**
Don't ask "do you want this?" Ask: *"Are you ready to plug these revenue leaks before the wedding season hits?"*

## 🛡️ Objection Handling

**"We already get work through word of mouth."**
"Word of mouth is great — but it has a ceiling. Every bride or corporate planner who hears about you still Googles you before calling. Right now when they search, nothing comes up. A website gives word of mouth somewhere to land."

**"It's expensive."**
"One mid-sized wedding catering event is anywhere from ₹50,000 to ₹3 Lakhs. If this infrastructure brings in just ONE new wedding inquiry that converts, the website pays for itself immediately. After that, it's pure margin."

**"We'll think about it."**
"Completely fair. Just keep in mind that the ₹5,000 launch discount and the waived setup fees for the WhatsApp automation are specifically tied to starting this week so we can deliver before the wedding season rush."

**"Someone I know can build it."**
"That's totally fine. Anyone can write code. But we don't just build websites; we build revenue-generating digital infrastructures that actually capture wedding leads. I'd be happy to show you a live reference of our work."
